Ilja

Ilja was the sweetest and most adorable cat on this planet. He was born around April/May 2007. We both met in February 2008 in an animal shelter. We were told that he's a very anxious cat and would never allow to be petted. It took him only 3 months to become the greatest cuddle bear.

We spent each day close to each other. I watched him sleeping or watching Cat TV (=looking out the window), he followed me around or slept on the desk when I was working.

This sweetest boy left this world and me suddenly and too soon on October 31st 2018.
