Angelo

Angelo is the only girl in the bunch. She started to hang around a couple of weeks after Ilja passed away. I soon found out that she was left behind by her first family (humans suck!). When she and I met, she was living on the street for 4 years. A broken tooth and ear mites made some visits to the vet (whom she now wholeheartedly hates) necessary.

Today, she enjoys life to the fullest. She spends her day sleeping in bed, on the dresser, my computer (while I'm working!) or the book I'm reading (while I'm reading). In between her naps she's having luxurious snacks, watching Cat TV or mice on her favorite Youtube channel. She also likes to wake me up at 3 am because an empty bowl means imminent starving.
