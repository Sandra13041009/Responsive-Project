Pepe

Pepe is the newest member to our group. He observed Angelo and me for quite some time from across the street before he decided to get closer. He was a stray cat for many years and was abused and shot by some of the villagers. So it's no surprise Pepe was suspicious of humans.

For reasons unknown to me, he asked me for help when he was badly injured by a trap. Ever since these days, he stays with me. Pepe loves playing, climbing trees and - yes - cuddling. Pepe is FIV+ and suffers from Leucosis. Whatever time we have left, he will have the best time a cat can have!
