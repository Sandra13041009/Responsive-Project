Pepe

Angelo is the only girl in the bunch. She started to hang around a couple of weeks after Ilja passed away. I soon found out that she was left behind by her first family (humans suck!). When she and I met, she was living on the street for 4 years.

Today, she enjoys life to the fullest. She spends her day sleeping in bed, on the dresser, my computer (while I'm working!) or the book I'm reading. In between her naps she's having luxurious snacks, watching Cat TV or mice on her favorite Youtube channel. She is a sweet darling and I'm glad she's here.
